Energy-Saving Suggestions

AC Temp Settings

Keep your Air Conditioner at 24°C (75°F) to save up to 18% electricity.

Phantom Loads

Unplug chargers and stand-by electronics. Phantom power accounts for 5-10% of energy bills.

LED Lighting Upgrade

Switch from incandescent bulbs to LED. LEDs consume 75% less energy and last 25x longer.
